About

My name is Vadr and I am a photographer. I have been in the business for over 4 years and I have a passion for capturing the beauty of the world around me. I specialize in family photographs, safari photographs, graduation photos, and editorial photography. I am dedicated to providing my clients with the best possible service and I am committed to capturing the moments that matter most to them. If you are looking for a photographer who is professional, reliable, and passionate about their work, then look no further than Vadr Pics.
