About

My name is Vadr, and I am a professional photographer with over 4 years of experience. I specialize in capturing the beauty of the world through family portraits, safari adventures, graduation milestones, and editorial photography.

Biography

Over the years, I've honed my skills in various photography styles. My goal is to preserve your cherished moments and bring your vision to life through the lens of my camera. these skill have also allowed me to pass on my knowledge to the next generation of photographers through workshops and seminars. The photography firm has also created a hub of photographers who share the same concept of preserving memories and creating art through photography, thus it has attracted members to oin VADR PICS and together we are working on various projects to showcase our work to the world.
